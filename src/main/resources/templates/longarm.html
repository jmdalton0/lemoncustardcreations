Edge to Edge Longarm Quilting Service

I offer edge-to-edge (E2E) longarm quilting to beautifully finish your quilt top with a continuous, all-over design. I am currently offering to bind your quilt for FREE if there is enough backing fabric left over. This style of quilting provides texture, durability, and a polished look while allowing your piecing to shine.

Edge-to-edge quilting uses a single repeated pattern stitched seamlessly across the entire quilt surface, making it a popular and cost-effective option for both everyday quilts and gifts.

What's Included

  • Your quilt top
  • Hobbs 80/20 batting
  • Trimmed and ready for binding if requested.
