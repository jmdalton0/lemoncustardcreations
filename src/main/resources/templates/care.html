Important Stuff

Materials:

  • Top: 100% cotton fabric
  • Batting: soft cotton or cotton-blend batting for warmth without heaviness
  • Backing: coordinating 100% cotton fabric or soft Minky/Cuddle

Care Instructions:

All quilts:

  • Machine wash cold on gentle cycle
  • Use mild detergent
  • Tumble dry low or lay flat to dry
  • Do not bleach

Minky/Cuddle backed quilts:

  • No fabric softener
  • Tumble dry, no heat
  • No dryer sheets
