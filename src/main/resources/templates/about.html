Who is Lemon Custard Creations?

Hi, I'm Kari — owner, creator, quilter, and not-so-great photographer behind Lemon Custard Creations.

If you have an idea or are dreaming up something special, I'd love to help bring it to life. Reach out anytime at lemoncustardco@gmail.com.

Why the name Lemon Custard Creations? EVERYONE asks me that. The short answer: why not? The long answer: who doesn't love lemon custard? It's tart (like me), and bright and cheery (like my quilts). Yellow is a happy, vibrant, fun color, and who doesn't want to feel that way every day?

Plus, if you quilt, you know the lingo: jelly rolls, layer cakes, charm packs, fat quarters, dessert rolls, fat eighths, honey buns, and quilter's candy. We sound like sugar addicts. What we really are is addicted to fabric — and to creating.

HUGE kudos to my adorable daughter for designing the luscious lemon logo, as well as to my son for creating this amazing webpage for me! I truly have the best children.

I'm located in Henderson, Nevada.
